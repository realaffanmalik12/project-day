Overview

The Hyundai Elantra N Line represents the perfect fusion of everyday practicality and exhilarating performance. Powered by a turbocharged 1.6-liter engine delivering 201 horsepower, the N Line offers genuine sports sedan dynamics while maintaining impressive fuel efficiency at 33 MPG on the highway.

The N Line trim elevates the Elantra experience with aggressive styling cues, including a unique front grille, sporty side skirts, and a distinctive rear spoiler. Inside, sport bucket seats with N Line branding, a leather-wrapped steering wheel, and metal pedals create an authentic performance atmosphere. The advanced 10.25-inch touchscreen infotainment system with wireless smartphone integration keeps you connected on every drive.
