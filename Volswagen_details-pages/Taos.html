Overview

The Volkswagen Jetta delivers an exceptional balance of spirited performance and remarkable fuel economy. Under the hood, the 1.5-liter turbocharged engine produces 158 horsepower while achieving an impressive 42 MPG on the highway, making it perfect for both daily commutes and weekend adventures.

Step inside the Jetta and discover a cabin that rivals luxury sedans. Premium materials throughout, supportive sport seats with available heating, and generous rear legroom make every journey comfortable. The advanced infotainment system features a responsive touchscreen, wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, and premium audio that transforms your daily commute into a concert experience.
