Overview

The Audi A3 epitomizes the perfect synthesis of premium craftsmanship and dynamic performance. Its turbocharged 2.0-liter engine delivers an exhilarating 201 horsepower, propelling you from 0 to 60 mph in just 5.9 seconds, while maintaining an impressive 34 MPG highway fuel economy. The legendary quattro all-wheel drive system ensures confident handling in any weather condition.

Step inside to experience the renowned Audi interior quality, where every surface reflects meticulous attention to detail. The available Virtual Cockpit displays critical information on a customizable 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster, while the MMI touch infotainment system with 10.1-inch display keeps you seamlessly connected. Premium materials, ambient lighting, and sport seats create an environment that transforms every drive into a first-class experience.
