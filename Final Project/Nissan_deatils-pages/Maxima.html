Overview

The Nissan Altima redefines what a midsize sedan can be with its perfect blend of striking design, intelligent technology, and efficient performance. Powered by a responsive 2.5-liter engine producing 188 horsepower, the Altima delivers confident acceleration while achieving an impressive 39 MPG on the highway, making every journey both thrilling and economical.

Step into a cabin designed around you, featuring premium materials, available leather-appointed seats, and an intuitive layout that puts everything within easy reach. The advanced Nissan Intelligent Mobility suite includes ProPILOT Assist, which combines intelligent cruise control with steering assist to make highway driving more relaxing. The available 8-inch touchscreen with wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto keeps you connected while the available Bose Premium Audio system transforms your commute into a concert hall.
