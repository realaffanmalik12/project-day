Overview

The Ford F-150 continues its legendary reign as America's best-selling truck, combining unmatched capability with cutting-edge technology. The available 3.5L EcoBoost V6 engine delivers a commanding 400 horsepower and 500 lb-ft of torque, enabling a best-in-class towing capacity of up to 14,000 pounds. The high-strength, military-grade aluminum alloy body provides exceptional durability while improving fuel efficiency to 24 MPG highway.

Inside, the F-150 offers a work-ready yet refined interior with available leather seating, a massive 12-inch touchscreen running SYNC 4, and clever storage solutions throughout. The available Pro Power Onboard system transforms your truck into a mobile generator with up to 7.2kW of exportable power. Whether you're hauling equipment to a job site or heading out for a weekend adventure, the F-150 delivers uncompromising capability with modern comfort.
