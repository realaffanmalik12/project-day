Overview

The Mercedes-Benz C-Class represents the pinnacle of luxury sedan engineering, combining sophisticated German craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology. The 2.0-liter turbocharged engine delivers an exhilarating 255 horsepower while maintaining impressive fuel efficiency at 35 MPG highway, proving that performance and economy can coexist in perfect harmony.

Step into a cabin where every detail exudes elegance and refinement. Premium Nappa leather upholstery, handcrafted wood trim, and ambient lighting with 64 colors create an atmosphere of unparalleled luxury. The 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster and 11.9-inch central touchscreen showcase the latest MBUX infotainment system, featuring natural voice control and augmented reality navigation that transforms every journey into an experience.
