Overview

The 2022 Toyota Corolla continues its legendary reign as the world's best-selling car, offering unmatched reliability, exceptional fuel efficiency, and outstanding value. Powered by a proven 1.8-liter engine producing 139 horsepower, the Corolla delivers smooth, confident performance while achieving an impressive 40 MPG on the highway, making it the perfect companion for budget-conscious drivers who refuse to compromise on quality.

Inside the Corolla, you'll find a surprisingly upscale cabin with soft-touch materials, intuitive controls, and Toyota's latest infotainment technology. The 8-inch touchscreen features wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, while the available premium JBL audio system ensures every drive is accompanied by crystal-clear sound. With Toyota's reputation for building vehicles that last for decades and retain their value better than competitors, the Corolla represents one of the smartest automotive investments you can make.
