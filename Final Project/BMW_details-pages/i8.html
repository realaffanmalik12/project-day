Overview

The BMW 3 Series continues to set the standard for sports sedans worldwide, combining exhilarating performance with refined luxury. The turbocharged 2.0-liter engine delivers 255 horsepower and propels the 3 Series from 0 to 60 mph in just 5.5 seconds, while achieving an impressive 36 MPG on the highway. The perfectly balanced 50/50 weight distribution and legendary BMW driving dynamics deliver unmatched handling precision.

Inside, the 3 Series cabin showcases BMW's commitment to driver-focused luxury. The latest iDrive 8 system features dual displays including a 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster and a 14.9-inch curved touchscreen that seamlessly integrates all vehicle functions. Premium materials, ergonomic sport seats, and available Vernasca leather create an environment that elevates every journey from mundane to memorable.
