Overview

The 2021 Honda Civic continues its legacy as one of America's most trusted compact sedans, combining reliability, efficiency, and engaging driving dynamics. Powered by a responsive 2.0-liter naturally aspirated engine delivering 158 horsepower, the Civic achieves an impressive 38 MPG on the highway, making it an ideal choice for daily commuters and road trip enthusiasts alike.

Inside, the Civic offers a surprisingly spacious cabin with premium materials and thoughtful design. The driver-focused cockpit features a user-friendly infotainment system with a 7-inch touchscreen, Apple CarPlay and Android Auto compatibility, and excellent visibility from all angles. With its low cost of ownership, exceptional resale value, and Honda's renowned reliability, the Civic 2021 represents smart automotive value.
